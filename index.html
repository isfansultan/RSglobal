About Us

RS Global Trans Inc is a technology‑driven logistics platform designed to empower independent drivers and small fleets. Our network spans all 48 contiguous states, ensuring steady loads and consistent support no matter where you’re headed. With quick pay options, real‑time tracking and a team of experienced dispatchers, we make sure you spend less time waiting and more time on the road earning.
